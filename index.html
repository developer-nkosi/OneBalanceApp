About OneBalance →

OneBalance is a Malawi-based fintech super-app bridging the gap between Airtel Money and TNM Mpamba. We empower Malawians to manage their finances seamlessly across networks, all from a single interface.

Everything You Need, One App

One balance that works across all services

Universal Wallet

Deposit from Airtel Money or TNM Mpamba. Send money to anyone, regardless of their network.

Food & Rides

Order from your favorite restaurants or book verified rides with transparent pricing.

Digital Tickets

Buy tickets for shows, concerts, and sports with unique QR codes that prevent fraud.

Escrow Protection

Your money is held safely until service is delivered. 100% safe marketplace transactions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about OneBalance

Yes! We're the first app in Malawi with true interoperability. You can:

  • Deposit from either Airtel Money or TNM Mpamba
  • Send money to any Malawi number, regardless of network
  • Use one balance across all services
  • Withdraw to your preferred network

Withdrawing is simple:

  1. Go to "Wallet" section in the app
  2. Select "Withdraw"
  3. Choose destination (Airtel Money or TNM Mpamba)
  4. Enter amount and PIN
  5. Money arrives within minutes

Note: A small 1% fee applies (capped by RBM regulations)


We use escrow protection for all marketplace transactions:

  • Your payment is held securely when you order
  • The merchant only receives payment when you click "Received"
  • If food doesn't arrive within estimated time, you can cancel for full refund
  • For quality issues, contact support within 1 hour for resolution

Business registration is simple:

  1. Fill the merchant form on our website
  2. Our team will contact you within 48 hours
  3. Submit required documents (business registration, ID, etc.)
  4. Complete brief training on merchant app
  5. Get activated and start receiving orders!

Documents needed: Business certificate, TPIN, National ID, proof of address
