Our Story

Founded in 2024, OneBalance was born from a simple observation: Malawians were tired of switching SIM cards just to send money.

We witnessed daily struggles with mobile money interoperability — people carrying multiple phones, missing payments, and paying unnecessary fees because Airtel Money couldn't easily talk to TNM Mpamba.

We asked: What if there was one app that worked with ALL networks? One balance for everything — sending money, ordering food, booking rides, buying tickets?

Today, OneBalance is Malawi's first truly interoperable super-app, licensed by the Reserve Bank of Malawi and trusted by thousands.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To break down financial barriers in Malawi by creating an inclusive digital ecosystem that works for everyone, regardless of their mobile network.

We believe in:

  • Financial inclusion for all Malawians
  • Technology that solves real problems
  • Building trust through transparency
  • Driving economic growth through innovation

Our Vision

To become Malawi's preferred digital lifestyle platform, where every transaction happens seamlessly in one place.

By 2030, we aim to:

  • Serve 1 million active users
  • Partner with 10,000+ merchants
  • Process MWK 1 billion daily
  • Expand to all major cities in Malawi

Our Journey

January 2024

Idea Born

Concept developed after observing network interoperability challenges in Lilongwe markets.

March 2024

Company Registered

OneBalance Digital Solutions Ltd officially registered with Registrar General.

June 2024

RBM Sandbox Entry

Accepted into Reserve Bank of Malawi's Regulatory Sandbox for testing.

September 2024

Beta Launch

First 1,000 users onboarded in Lilongwe. Processed MWK 5 million in first month.

December 2024

RBM License Approved

Received full Payment Service Provider license from Reserve Bank of Malawi.

Today

Growing Strong

Serving 50,000+ users, 1,200+ merchants, processing MWK 250+ million monthly.
